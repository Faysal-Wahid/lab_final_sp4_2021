What is our E-tech ecommerce

E-commerce (electronic commerce) is the buying and selling of goods and services, or the transmitting of funds or data, over an electronic network, primarily the internet. These business transactions occur either as business-to-business (B2B), business-to-consumer (B2C), consumer-to-consumer or consumer-to-business. E-commerce is powered by the internet, where customers can access an online store to browse through, and place orders for products or services via their own devices. It's a faster process very flexible for customers.
